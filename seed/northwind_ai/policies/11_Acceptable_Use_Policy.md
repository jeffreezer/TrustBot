# Northwind AI — Acceptable Use Policy

> **SYNTHETIC / FICTIONAL demo document for TrustBot.** Consistent with the Northwind profile and controls.
> **Owner:** Head of GRC · **Version:** 2.0 · **Approved:** 2025-05-15 · **Review cadence:** Annual · **Next review:** 2026-05-15
> **Classification:** Internal
> **Related controls:** CC1.1, HR1.2

## 1. Purpose

Define acceptable use of Northwind systems, devices, and data by personnel.

## 2. Scope

All employees and contractors using Northwind systems or accessing Northwind/customer data.

## 3. Policy

- Company systems and accounts are used for authorized business purposes.
- Users protect their credentials, use MFA, and never share accounts.
- Confidential and customer data is accessed only on a need-to-know basis and handled per the Data Classification & Handling Policy.
- Company devices are managed (MDM), encrypted, and run endpoint protection (EDR); users must not disable security controls.
- Prohibited: attempting to bypass security controls, installing unauthorized software, or using company systems for unlawful activity.
- Suspected security incidents must be reported promptly to security@northwind.ai.

## 4. Roles & Responsibilities

- **All users:** comply with this policy.
- **IT/Security:** enforce and monitor compliance.

## 5. Enforcement

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination.
