## 1. Purpose

Address security across the employment lifecycle: before, during, and after employment.

## 2. Scope

All employees and, where applicable, contractors.

## 3. Policy

- **Pre-employment.** Background checks are performed where permitted by law prior to start. Confidentiality/NDA terms are signed at hire.
- **During employment.** All personnel complete **security awareness training at hire and annually**; phishing simulations are run periodically. Role-specific security training is provided where relevant (e.g., secure coding for engineers).
- **Acceptable use.** Personnel acknowledge the Acceptable Use Policy.
- **Termination/transfer.** Access is revoked promptly on termination or role change (see Access Control Policy); company assets are returned.
- **Disciplinary process.** Security violations are handled through a documented disciplinary process.

## 4. Roles & Responsibilities

- **HR:** owns background checks, agreements, and the disciplinary process.
- **Security:** owns training content and phishing simulations.

## 5. Exceptions

Exceptions require documented approval from HR and the CISO.
