## 1. Purpose

Define how Northwind identifies, assesses, treats, and monitors information security risk.

## 2. Scope

Risks to the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of Northwind systems and customer data, including vendor and operational risks.

## 3. Policy

- **Risk assessment.** A formal risk assessment is performed at least **annually** and when significant changes occur.
- **Risk register.** Identified risks are documented in a risk register with owner, likelihood, impact, and treatment.
- **Treatment.** Risks are mitigated, transferred, avoided, or accepted; accepted risks require documented approval.
- **Monitoring.** Treatment progress is tracked to remediation and reviewed by GRC.
- **Alignment.** The program aligns to ISO/IEC 27001 risk management and SOC 2 criteria.

## 4. Roles & Responsibilities

- **Head of GRC:** owns the risk program and register.
- **Risk owners:** execute treatment plans.
- **Leadership:** approves risk acceptance for significant risks.

## 5. Exceptions

Risk acceptance is the documented exception mechanism and requires Head of GRC (and, for significant risk, executive) approval.
