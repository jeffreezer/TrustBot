> **Related controls:** P3.2, P6.1, DSP (catalog)

## 1. Purpose

Define how Northwind protects personal data and honors privacy obligations.

## 2. Scope

Personal data processed by Northwind on behalf of customers and Northwind's own personnel/customer account data.

## 3. Policy

- **Program alignment.** The privacy program aligns to **ISO/IEC 27701** and supports applicable data protection requirements (e.g., GDPR-style data subject rights).
- **Roles.** Northwind acts as a **processor** for customer content and a **controller** for its own account/business data; responsibilities are defined accordingly.
- **Model training.** Enterprise and API customer content is **not used to train Northwind base models by default**; training use is strictly opt-in.
- **Data subject / customer requests.** Access, correction, and deletion requests are supported within contractual timeframes.
- **Data residency.** An EU region is available for customers requiring EU data residency.
- **Confidentiality.** Customer content is treated as Confidential (see Data Classification & Handling Policy) and retained/deleted per the Data Retention & Deletion Policy.
- **Subprocessors.** Personal data shared with subprocessors is governed by DPAs (see Vendor / Third-Party Risk Management Policy).

## 4. Roles & Responsibilities

- **DPO:** owns the privacy program and data-subject request handling.
- **Engineering:** implements privacy and data-protection controls.

## 5. Exceptions

Exceptions require documented approval from the DPO and Legal.
